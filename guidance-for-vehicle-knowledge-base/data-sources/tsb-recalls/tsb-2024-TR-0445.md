# TSB 2024-TR-0445: Harsh 1-2 Shift Under Light Throttle

**Applies to:** 2021-2024 RAM ProMaster 3.6L

## Condition
Transmission may exhibit a firm 1-2 upshift at light throttle between 15-25 mph.

## Correction
Reflash TCM with updated calibration (software level AA). No parts required. Labor: 0.5 hours.

## Warranty Coverage
Powertrain warranty (5yr/60k).

## Driver Communication
> "There's a known issue with your vehicle that matches what you're describing. A Technical Service Bulletin has been issued with a fix. This should be covered under your warranty. Would you like me to schedule the repair?"
