# TSB 2024-EL-0089: Infotainment Screen Intermittent Black

**Applies to:** 2022-2024 Chevrolet Equinox

## Condition
Touchscreen may go black intermittently while driving. Audio continues to function.

## Correction
Replace infotainment display module (GM P/N 84983018). Requires recalibration after install. Labor: 1.5 hours.

## Warranty Coverage
Bumper-to-bumper (3yr/36k).

## Driver Communication
> "There's a known issue with your vehicle that matches what you're describing. A Technical Service Bulletin has been issued with a fix. Let me check your warranty coverage. Would you like me to schedule the repair?"
