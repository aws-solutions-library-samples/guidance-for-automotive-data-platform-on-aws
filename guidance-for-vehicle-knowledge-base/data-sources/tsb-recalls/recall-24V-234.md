# NHTSA Safety Recall 24V-234

## Brake Booster Vacuum Hose May Disconnect

**Affected Vehicles:** 2022-2023 Ford Transit Connect
**Date Issued:** 2024-02-15
**Estimated Affected:** 45,000 vehicles

## Defect Description
The brake booster vacuum hose may disconnect from the intake manifold fitting, resulting in reduced brake assist. Increased pedal effort required to stop.

## Remedy
Dealers will install a revised hose clamp and inspect the hose for damage. Replace hose if cracked. Repair time: 0.5 hours.

## Interim Driver Guidance
If brake pedal feels hard or requires excessive force, pull over safely. Vehicle can still be stopped but requires significantly more pedal pressure.

## Driver Communication
> "Your vehicle is affected by a safety recall — 24V-234. The brake booster vacuum hose may disconnect from the intake manifold fitting, resulting in reduced brake assist. The repair is free at any authorized dealer. Would you like me to help schedule an appointment?"
