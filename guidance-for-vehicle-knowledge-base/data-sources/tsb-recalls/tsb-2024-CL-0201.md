# TSB 2024-CL-0201: A/C Compressor Clutch Engagement Noise

**Applies to:** 2021-2024 Chevrolet Equinox

## Condition
Single click/clunk when A/C compressor engages, especially noticeable at idle.

## Correction
Install compressor clutch damper kit (GM P/N 84612987). No compressor replacement needed. Labor: 0.8 hours.

## Warranty Coverage
Bumper-to-bumper (3yr/36k).

## Driver Communication
> "There's a known issue with your vehicle that matches what you're describing. A Technical Service Bulletin has been issued with a fix. Let me check your warranty coverage. Would you like me to schedule the repair?"
