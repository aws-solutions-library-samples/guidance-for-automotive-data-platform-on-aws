# TSB 2023-SU-0334: Front Strut Mount Clunk Over Bumps

**Applies to:** 2019-2022 Ford Escape

## Condition
Clunking noise from front suspension when driving over bumps at low speed.

## Correction
Replace front strut mount bearings with updated design (Ford P/N LJ6Z-18183-A). Labor: 2.0 hours per side.

## Warranty Coverage
Bumper-to-bumper (3yr/36k). Ford Extended Service Plan covers if purchased.

## Driver Communication
> "There's a known issue with your vehicle that matches what you're describing. A Technical Service Bulletin has been issued with a fix. Let me check your warranty coverage. Would you like me to schedule the repair?"
