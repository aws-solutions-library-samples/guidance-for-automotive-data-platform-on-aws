# TSB 2024-EN-0112: Engine Oil Consumption Above Normal

**Applies to:** 2019-2023 Chevrolet Equinox 1.5T

## Condition
Some vehicles may consume more than 1 quart of oil per 3,000 miles due to piston ring design.

## Correction
Replace piston rings with updated design (GM P/N 12710344). Requires engine partial disassembly. Labor: 8.5 hours.

## Warranty Coverage
Powertrain warranty (5yr/60k) or Customer Satisfaction Program 22-NA-189 (7yr/84k).

## Driver Communication
> "There's a known issue with your vehicle that matches what you're describing. A Technical Service Bulletin has been issued with a fix. This should be covered under your warranty. Would you like me to schedule the repair?"
