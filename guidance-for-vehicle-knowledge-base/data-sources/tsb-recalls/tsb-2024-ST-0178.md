# TSB 2024-ST-0178: Power Steering Assist Reduced Message

**Applies to:** 2020-2023 Chevrolet Equinox

## Condition
Driver may see 'Power Steering Assist Reduced' message with DTC C0545. Steering still functions but requires more effort.

## Correction
Replace steering column torque sensor (GM P/N 84754321). Requires steering column partial disassembly and recalibration. Labor: 2.5 hours.

## Warranty Coverage
Powertrain warranty (5yr/60k) — steering is covered under powertrain on this platform.

## Driver Communication
> "There's a known issue with your vehicle that matches what you're describing. A Technical Service Bulletin has been issued with a fix. This should be covered under your warranty. Would you like me to schedule the repair?"
