# Battery and Power System Remediation Playbook

This playbook provides diagnostic procedures, remediation strategies, and customer communication templates for common battery and power system issues in electric vehicles.

---

## Supplier Batch Defect Issues

### Issue 1: Early Battery Health Degradation

**Symptoms:**
- Battery health drops below 80% within the first 12 months of ownership
- Typically affects specific production batches
- May be accompanied by range anxiety complaints

**Severity:** Critical

**Root Cause:** Supplier batch defect in battery cells or thermal management components

**Recommended Action:**
Proactive battery replacement under warranty
- **Success Rate:** 94%
- **Average Resolution Time:** 3 days
- **Revenue Recovery Rate:** 89%

**Escalation Trigger:**
No improvement after replacement - escalate to engineering for batch analysis

**Customer Communication Template:**
> "We've identified a potential issue with your battery and are scheduling a complimentary replacement at your convenience. This proactive service ensures your vehicle maintains optimal performance and range."

---

### Issue 2: Accelerated Range Loss

**Symptoms:**
- Range loss exceeds 25% within 4 months
- Progressive degradation pattern
- Customer reports significant deviation from EPA estimates

**Severity:** High

**Root Cause:** Supplier batch defect affecting battery chemistry or cell balancing

**Recommended Action:**
Battery diagnostic + expedited replacement scheduling
- **Success Rate:** 91%
- **Average Resolution Time:** 5 days
- **Revenue Recovery Rate:** 85%

**Escalation Trigger:**
Continued degradation post-diagnostic - may indicate systemic issue requiring recall assessment

**Customer Communication Template:**
> "We noticed your vehicle may benefit from a battery health check. We'd like to schedule a no-cost inspection to ensure you're getting the best possible range and performance."

---

## Battery Management System (BMS) Malfunctions

### Issue 3: Charging Performance Degradation

**Symptoms:**
- Charge time increases by more than 40% compared to baseline
- Slow charging even on high-power chargers
- No physical damage to charging port

**Severity:** High

**Root Cause:** BMS firmware bug or calibration drift

**Recommended Action:**
BMS firmware update + diagnostic
- **Success Rate:** 87%
- **Average Resolution Time:** 1 day
- **Revenue Recovery Rate:** 82%

**Escalation Trigger:**
Symptoms persist post-update - may require BMS hardware replacement

**Customer Communication Template:**
> "We have a software update available that addresses charging performance. This can be done remotely or at your nearest service center, typically completed within an hour."

---

### Issue 4: Charging System Errors

**Symptoms:**
- Charging port errors displayed on dashboard
- BMS diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs)
- Intermittent charging failures

**Severity:** Critical

**Root Cause:** BMS hardware malfunction affecting charge controller communication

**Recommended Action:**
BMS replacement + charging system repair
- **Success Rate:** 89%
- **Average Resolution Time:** 4 days
- **Revenue Recovery Rate:** 86%

**Escalation Trigger:**
Multiple error codes recurring after repair - investigate electrical system integrity

**Customer Communication Template:**
> "We've detected a system issue requiring service. We're prioritizing your appointment to minimize any inconvenience and ensure safe, reliable charging."

---

## Thermal Management Failures

### Issue 5: Battery Overheating

**Symptoms:**
- Overheating warnings on dashboard
- Capacity loss during or after high-temperature events
- Reduced performance in warm weather

**Severity:** Critical

**Root Cause:** Thermal management system failure (coolant leak, pump failure, or thermal paste degradation)

**Recommended Action:**
Cooling system inspection + thermal paste replacement
- **Success Rate:** 91%
- **Average Resolution Time:** 2 days
- **Revenue Recovery Rate:** 88%

**Escalation Trigger:**
Recurring thermal events within 30 days - may indicate design flaw requiring engineering review

**Customer Communication Template:**
> "We'd like to perform a preventive cooling system service to ensure optimal battery performance and longevity. This service helps maintain your vehicle's efficiency in all weather conditions."

---

### Issue 6: Temperature-Dependent Charging Issues

**Symptoms:**
- Reduced charging speed specifically in warm conditions
- Normal charging performance in moderate temperatures
- No overheating warnings

**Severity:** Medium

**Root Cause:** Thermal management software calibration error

**Recommended Action:**
Thermal management software calibration
- **Success Rate:** 93%
- **Average Resolution Time:** 1 day
- **Revenue Recovery Rate:** 79%

**Escalation Trigger:**
No improvement after calibration - investigate hardware thermal sensors

**Customer Communication Template:**
> "A quick software calibration can optimize your charging in various temperatures. This update is available over-the-air and takes just a few minutes to complete."

## Service Pattern Issues

### Issue 7: Excessive Service Visits

**Symptoms:**
- Customer has made 3 or more visits within 90 days for the same issue
- Issue remains unresolved or recurs quickly
- Customer satisfaction at risk

**Severity:** High

**Root Cause:** Misdiagnosis, incomplete repair, or complex systemic issue

**Recommended Action:**
Root cause escalation + dedicated case manager assignment
- **Success Rate:** 78%
- **Average Resolution Time:** 14 days
- **Revenue Recovery Rate:** 71%

**Escalation Trigger:**
Issue unresolved after escalation - consider vehicle buyback or extended warranty

**Customer Communication Template:**
> "We're assigning a dedicated specialist to resolve your concern and ensure you don't need to return. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we're committed to finding a permanent solution."

---

### Issue 8: Recurring Component Failures

**Symptoms:**
- Same component has failed 2 or more times
- Pattern suggests design or manufacturing defect
- Customer losing confidence in vehicle reliability

**Severity:** Critical

**Root Cause:** Component design flaw, installation error, or environmental factor

**Recommended Action:**
Component replacement + engineering review
- **Success Rate:** 85%
- **Average Resolution Time:** 7 days
- **Revenue Recovery Rate:** 83%

**Escalation Trigger:**
Third occurrence of same failure - initiate recall assessment and customer retention program

**Customer Communication Template:**
> "We're replacing the affected component and our engineering team is reviewing to prevent recurrence. We stand behind our vehicles and are committed to ensuring long-term reliability."

## Usage Guidelines

**For Service Advisors:**
1. Match customer symptoms to playbook entries
2. Follow recommended action sequence
3. Use provided communication templates
4. Monitor for escalation triggers
5. Document outcomes for continuous improvement

**For Customer Success Teams:**
1. Proactively reach out to customers matching symptom patterns
2. Schedule service before customer experiences frustration
3. Use templates as starting point, personalize for customer context
4. Track success rates and adjust strategies

**For Engineering Teams:**
1. Review escalated cases for systemic patterns
2. Update playbook based on new learnings
3. Provide feedback on success rates and resolution times
4. Identify opportunities for preventive measures
